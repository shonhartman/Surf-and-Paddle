Detroit

a field guide

»Welcome«

My first visit to Detroit was last January. A cruel time of year, for sure. There's a dry, frozen-river cold that sinks into your bones. You imagine those first auto factories and you just shiver. Luckily, the city is gifted with wonderful corner bars like Abick's and The Bronx, and for those first few trips they warmed me up. Before January, my Detroit was a mixture of Pistons basketball, Elmore Leonard, a dozen Motown choruses, and of late, the shock-value headlines that we'd all read. Detroit Calls It Quits.
